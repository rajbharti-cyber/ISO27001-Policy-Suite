# 🔐 Access Control Policy

## 📌 Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish requirements for managing user access to organizational systems, applications, networks, and sensitive information assets.

This policy supports:
- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Accountability
- Regulatory compliance
- Least privilege enforcement

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Ensure authorized access to systems and data
- Prevent unauthorized access
- Reduce insider threat risk
- Support audit and compliance requirements
- Strengthen identity and access governance

---

# 👥 Scope

This policy applies to:
- Employees
- Contractors
- Third-party vendors
- Privileged administrators
- Remote users
- All organizational systems and applications

---

# 🛡️ Policy Requirements

## 1️⃣ User Access Management
- Access must be approved by authorized management.
- User accounts must be unique and attributable to individuals.
- Shared accounts should be prohibited unless formally approved.

---

## 2️⃣ Least Privilege
- Users must receive only the minimum access required.
- Privileged access must be restricted and monitored.

---

## 3️⃣ Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
MFA must be enabled for:
- Privileged accounts
- Remote access
- Critical business applications

---

## 4️⃣ Password Management
Passwords must:
- Meet organizational complexity standards
- Be securely stored
- Be periodically reviewed where applicable

---

## 5️⃣ Access Reviews
Periodic access reviews must be performed to:
- Validate active users
- Remove excessive access
- Identify dormant accounts
- Verify role appropriateness

---

## 6️⃣ Joiner-Mover-Leaver (JML) Process
Access provisioning and deprovisioning must align with:
- Employee onboarding
- Role changes
- Employment termination

---

## 7️⃣ Logging & Monitoring
Access-related activities should be logged and monitored, including:
- Authentication events
- Privileged actions
- Failed login attempts
- Access modifications

---

# 📋 Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| IT Security Team | Access governance oversight |
| System Owners | Access approval and validation |
| Managers | User access authorization |
| Users | Compliance with access requirements |
| Audit & Compliance Teams | Review control effectiveness |

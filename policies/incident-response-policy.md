# 🚨 Incident Response Policy

## 📌 Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish a structured approach for identifying, reporting, managing, and responding to information security incidents.

This policy supports:
- Rapid incident response
- Business continuity
- Regulatory compliance
- Risk reduction
- Governance accountability

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Detect and respond to security incidents effectively
- Minimize operational disruption
- Reduce financial and reputational impact
- Support regulatory and audit requirements
- Improve organizational resilience

---

# 👥 Scope

This policy applies to:
- Employees
- Contractors
- Third-party service providers
- IT systems
- Cloud environments
- Organizational networks and applications

---

# 🛡️ Incident Management Requirements

## 1️⃣ Incident Identification
Security incidents may include:
- Unauthorized access
- Malware infections
- Data leakage
- Phishing attacks
- Insider threats
- System compromise
- Service disruption

---

## 2️⃣ Incident Reporting
All suspected security incidents must be:
- Reported immediately
- Logged through approved channels
- Escalated to the appropriate response team

---

## 3️⃣ Incident Classification
Incidents should be classified based on:
- Severity
- Business impact
- Data sensitivity
- Regulatory implications
- Operational disruption

---

## 4️⃣ Containment & Remediation
Response teams should:
- Contain affected systems
- Prevent further impact
- Investigate root cause
- Implement remediation actions

---

## 5️⃣ Communication & Escalation
Appropriate stakeholders must be notified, including:
- IT Security
- Management
- Compliance teams
- Legal teams (where applicable)
- Regulatory authorities (if required)

---

## 6️⃣ Evidence Preservation
Relevant logs, screenshots, and forensic evidence should be preserved to:
- Support investigations
- Maintain audit defensibility
- Enable post-incident review

---

## 7️⃣ Post-Incident Review
Post-incident activities should include:
- Lessons learned analysis
- Control improvement recommendations
- Governance reporting
- Process enhancements

---

# 📋 Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Security Team | Incident handling and investigation |
| IT Operations | Technical remediation support |
| Management | Decision-making and escalation |
| Compliance Team | Regulatory coordination |
| Employees | Timely incident reporting |
